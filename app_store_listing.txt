--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                              SHORT DESCRIPTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ElectroHub is a powerful, comprehensive electronics engineering toolkit that 
brings professional-grade calculation, analysis, and simulation tools to your 
fingertips. Perfect for engineers, students, hobbyists, and professionals.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                              FULL DESCRIPTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ElectroHub is the ultimate electronics engineering companion that combines 
multiple powerful tools into one seamless application. Whether you're designing 
circuits, analyzing signals, planning solar installations, or running virtual 
experiments, ElectroHub has everything you need.

Designed with both beginners and professionals in mind, ElectroHub provides 
intuitive interfaces for complex engineering calculations while maintaining 
the precision and accuracy that professionals demand.

Say goodbye to juggling multiple apps, spreadsheets, and calculators. 
ElectroHub consolidates all your electronics engineering needs into one 
beautiful, easy-to-use application that works on any device.
